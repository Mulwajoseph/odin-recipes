Ugali recipe

Ingredients

  • 7 pieces ofMatoke
  • Cooking Oil
  • A piece of Onion
  • 2 beef cubes
  • Spices
  • Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Clean the bananas and peel them then cut them into smaller pieces of your preffered size
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat,add onions and fry until translucent
  3. Add the diced tomatoes annd cook until they are mushy
  4. Add the Matoke,the beef cubes and all the spices
  5. Add water tha will cover the Matoke
  6. Mix well,cover and leave to cook for 25 minutes,Stirring halfway between until the matoke becomes tender
  7. Serve
  8. Enjoy
