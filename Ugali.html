Ugali Recipe

A plate of ugali

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Maize meal or corn meal
  • 2 cups of normal water

Instructions

  1. Heat water over medium heat in a deep pan
  2. Make sure the water comes to boil
  3. Add the maize meal and keep stirring with a strong wooden spoon
  4. Keep mixing the mixture as you add more flour until it becomes more thick while breaking the lumps
  5. When the mixture begins to come away from the pan,allow it to cook for 2-3 minutes
  6. Transfer the ugali to a platter and turn it upside down
  7. Enjoy pipping the hot ugali with your favourite stew
