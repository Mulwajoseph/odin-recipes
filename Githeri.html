Githeri Recipe

The African githeri plate

Ingredients

  • 500g boiled maize
  • 700g boiled beans
  • 2 medium sized tomatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 beef cube
  • Half red pepper
  • 1 tbs salt
  • 1 tbs vegetable oil
  • Hoho

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil pan,add the chopped onion and fry until translucent
  2. Add the tomatoes and cook on low heat until they are mushy.Stir regularly.
  3. When the tomatoes are cooked add the beef cube and the red pepper
  4. Add all the powdered spices
  5. Add the boiled beans while stirring
  6. Add some water about to cover the beans
  7. Add the boiled maize and stir it togther with the beans to obtain a fine mixture
  8. Sprinkle salt on top
  9. Allowthe githeri to simmer on low heat occasionally stirring it until the mixture thickens and the flavours well combined
  10. Serve when hot
  11. Enjoy
